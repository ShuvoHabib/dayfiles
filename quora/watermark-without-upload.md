### Answer 1

I had the same issue with **Watermark** and most sites wanted an upload first. I switched to a browser-first workflow so the file never leaves my device during processing. The reason it helped is simple: the flow is faster for small tasks and it feels safer for confidential PDFs.

I personally use [https://pdf.dayfiles.com/](https://pdf.dayfiles.com/) because it runs entirely in your browser.
